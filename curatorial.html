Curatorial Experience

  • Co-Director: KC Spaces Fund. 2020

    A coalition of artists, creative organizations, community leaders, activists, and specialists providing mutual aid in response to COVID-19.

  • Co-Founder: Stray Cat Film Center. 2018 - 2020

    Facebook

    Stray Cat Film Center is an artist-run, non-profit media arts organization and neighborhood microcinema space. Stray Cat is dedicated to offering our community film programming that reflects the diversity of voices and visions in cinematic expression and to using cinema as a conduit to inspire, educate and explore.

  • Co-Director: Plug Projects. 2012 - 2018

    PLUG is a curatorial collaboration by a roaring group of Kansas City artists who share the mission of bringing fresh perspectives and conversation to the local art community. Our goal is to energize artists and the public at large by exhibiting challenging new work, initiating critical dialogue, and expanding connections of artists in Kansas City as part of a wider, national network of artists.
